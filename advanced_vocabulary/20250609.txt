bundle (n) = a number of things tied or wrapped together; something that is wrapped up

inroad (n) = something that is achieved, especially by reducing the power or success of something else

wary (adj) = careful when dealing with somebody/something because you think that there may be a danger or problem

confectionery (n) = sweets, chocolate, etc.

perish (v) = to die, especially in a sudden violent way; to be lost or destroyed
